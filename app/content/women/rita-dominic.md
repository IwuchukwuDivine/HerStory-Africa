## Biography

Rita Uchenna Nkem Dominic Nwaturuocha was born on 12 July 1975 in Mbaise, Imo State, Nigeria. She studied Theatre Arts at the University of Port Harcourt and became one of Nollywood's most acclaimed actresses, known for roles in films such as *The Meeting*, *76*, and *Light in the Dark*.

She co-founded **The Audrey Silva Company**, a production company, and has produced and starred in films that have won national and international recognition. She has been a vocal advocate for better working conditions and professionalism in Nollywood and for women taking on producing and leadership roles.

## Historical Context

Nollywood has often been dominated by male producers and directors. Rita Dominic has used her success as an actress to move into production and to champion higher standards and more diverse stories, including those led by and about women.

## What She Fought For

Rita Dominic has fought for **quality and professionalism** in Nollywood and for **women's leadership** as producers and decision-makers. She has supported projects that tell nuanced stories about Nigerian women and society.

## Major Achievements

- Multiple AMVCA and other industry awards; leading actress in Nollywood
- Co-founder of The Audrey Silva Company; producer of acclaimed films
- Star of *The Meeting*, *76*, *Light in the Dark*, and many other films
- Advocate for women and professionalism in Nigerian film

## Her Impact Today

Rita Dominic is regarded as one of Nollywood's finest actresses and a trailblazer for women in production and leadership in the industry.
