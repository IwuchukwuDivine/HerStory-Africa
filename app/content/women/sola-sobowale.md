## Biography

Sola Sobowale was born in Ondo State, Nigeria. She trained as a teacher before turning to acting and became one of Nollywood's most recognisable and respected actresses. She is known for powerful performances in films such as **King of Boys** and **The Wedding Party** and for her work in television and theatre.

She has written and produced content and has been a mentor to younger actresses. She has spoken about the need for strong, complex roles for women and for veteran actresses to remain visible and influential in the industry.

## Historical Context

Nollywood has often favoured younger actresses for lead roles. Sola Sobowale has demonstrated that actresses can carry major films at any stage of their careers and has pushed for stories that centre older, authoritative women.

## What She Fought For

Sola Sobowale has fought for **strong female characters** and **visibility for veteran actresses** in Nollywood. She has advocated for better scripts and roles for women and for respect for experience in the industry.

## Major Achievements

- Star of *King of Boys*, *The Wedding Party*, and many other hit films and series
- Multiple AMVCA and other awards; one of Nollywood's most acclaimed actresses
- Writer and producer; mentor to younger actresses; advocate for women's roles

## Her Impact Today

Sola Sobowale is widely regarded as one of Nollywood's finest actresses and a trailblazer for complex, powerful roles for women in Nigerian film.
