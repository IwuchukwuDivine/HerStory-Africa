## About This Opportunity

The scholarship aims to empower young African women in STEM and integrate them into a pan-African community of women innovators. Recipients gain access to mentorship, professional growth opportunities, and community impact programs.

## What You Get

- $500 award (or local currency equivalent)
- Mentorship
- Professional growth opportunities

## Eligibility

- Female student of African origin, living and studying in Africa
- Enrolled in a Bachelor's program in a STEM-related field
- In 1st or 2nd year (or 3rd year for 5-year programs)
- Under 32 years of age
- Demonstrate academic excellence and financial need

## How to Apply

Submit the online application form, including essays detailing career goals and how the scholarship will help achieve them. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview and asked to submit two reference letters and current transcripts.
