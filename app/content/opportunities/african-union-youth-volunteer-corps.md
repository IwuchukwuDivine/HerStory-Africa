## About This Opportunity

The African Union Youth Volunteer Corps (AU-YVC) deploys young professionals to AU member states for approximately 12 months of service supporting development projects, institutions, and community initiatives aligned with Agenda 2063. This is volunteer service, not a corporate job, with a monthly stipend and strong emphasis on public service and Pan-African solidarity.

Ideal if you want field experience in governance, health, education, youth work, or related sectors with an AU-affiliated placement structure.

## What You Get

- Monthly stipend and health insurance
- Economy return ticket to deployment location
- Pre-deployment training and mentorship opportunities
- AU Service Passport and separation allowance upon completion
- Certificate of service and networking with African leaders

## Eligibility

- Ages 18 to 35 and a citizen of an AU member state (living on the continent or in the diaspora)
- Post-secondary qualification (TVET, Bachelor's, Master's, etc.)
- At least one year of verifiable volunteering experience and one year of professional work experience
- Proficient in at least one AU working language (Arabic, English, French, Kiswahili, Portuguese, or Spanish)
- Willing to work in another AU member state for 12 months

## How to Apply

Visit [jobs.au.int](https://jobs.au.int), search for the AUYVC post, and submit your application with an updated CV (maximum 3 pages, using the official AU CV template), passport or national ID, highest qualification, and a letter of recommendation.
