## About This Opportunity

Moniepoint's Women in Tech Internship (6th edition, themed "There is Space for You") is a paid, six-month program for a limited cohort of young women to work inside one of Africa's largest fintech companies. Interns join real teams spanning engineering, data, cybersecurity, product management, and design, with mentorship from practitioners shaping digital financial services at scale.

Applications opened on March 30, 2026. The program is built for women early in tech careers who want credible delivery experience in a regulated, high-growth environment.

## What You Get

- Six months of paid internship with structured onboarding
- Competitive salary, work tools, and branded merchandise
- Mentorship from industry experts across functional areas
- Live projects contributing to products and operations used in the market
- High-performing interns may be offered full-time employment

## Eligibility

- Women across Nigeria who are self-taught, formally trained, or transitioning into tech careers
- Ability to work full-time for the program duration
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills; prior projects or portfolios help for technical tracks

## How to Apply

Use the official Women in Tech page at [womenintech.moniepoint.com](https://womenintech.moniepoint.com/wit#about) for role descriptions and the application form. Roles include Cloud Engineering, Frontend and Backend Engineering, Data Engineering, Systems Administration, Product Management, Information Security, Mobile Engineering, and Site Reliability Engineering.
