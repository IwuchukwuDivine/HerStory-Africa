## About This Opportunity

This scholarship is part of Goldman Sachs' One Million Black Women Initiative, which aims to narrow opportunity gaps and positively impact the lives of Black women. Priority consideration is given to applicants attending an HBCU or the University of Miami.

## What You Get

- Up to $5,000 scholarship
- Educational opportunities
- Career development

## Eligibility

- Be an African American woman who is a US citizen or US permanent resident
- Have a minimum cumulative grade point average of 2.75 on a 4.0 scale
- Be enrolled as an incoming junior for the upcoming academic year at a US-located accredited 4-year college or university
- Have a declared major in a STEM/STEM-related discipline or a financial services discipline
- Have completed the FAFSA and have a demonstrated unmet financial aid need

## How to Apply

Applicants must complete the UNCF online application, including transcripts, FAFSA information, and other required documents.
