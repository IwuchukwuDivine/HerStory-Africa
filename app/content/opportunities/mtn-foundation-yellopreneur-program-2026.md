## About This Opportunity

The MTN Foundation runs this program to empower female entrepreneurs in Nigeria through entrepreneurship development. It focuses on training and funding to help scale sustainable enterprises.

## What You Get

- Business training and capacity building
- Access to capital
- Mentorship and ongoing advisory support

## Eligibility

- Nigerian female entrepreneurs

## How to Apply

Apply through the [official program webpage](https://www.mtn.ng/foundation/yellopreneur/). Complete the application form with details of your business and entrepreneurial plans.
