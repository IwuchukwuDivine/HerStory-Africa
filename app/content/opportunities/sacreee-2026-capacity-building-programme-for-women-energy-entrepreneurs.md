## About This Opportunity

SACREEE runs this capacity-building programme to advance the careers of women energy entrepreneurs in the Southern African Development Community (SADC) region. It focuses on training and networking without providing direct capital funding.

## What You Get

- 6-month virtual programme with roughly 8 hours per month commitment
- Business soft-skill training and engagement with industry role models
- Matchmaking with potential funders for enterprise financing

## Eligibility

- Women with SADC nationality who can commit to roughly 8 hours per month of virtual attendance
- Preferential consideration for those working in solar thermal, energy efficiency, and productive use of energy

## How to Apply

Submit applications via the [official webpage](https://www.sacreee.org/opportunity/call-applications-second-cohort-capacity-building-programme-women-energy-entrepreneurs). Commitment to the programme schedule is required.
