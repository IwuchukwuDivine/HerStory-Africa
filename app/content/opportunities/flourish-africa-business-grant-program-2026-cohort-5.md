## About This Opportunity

Flourish Africa runs this grant program to empower women-led startups in Nigeria through business and life skills development. It is designed for female-owned enterprises to gain investment readiness and growth support.

## What You Get

- Business and life skills training over 4 months
- Coaching, mentoring, and support to scale the business
- Grant funding for selected participants

## Eligibility

- Female entrepreneurs (at least 51 percent ownership if co-owned)
- Ages 18 to 50
- Business located in Nigeria and operational for at least 12 months
- Access to internet-enabled devices for virtual training

## How to Apply

Sign up and complete the application on the [official portal](https://oms.flourishafrica.com). Review eligibility and submit required information about the business.
