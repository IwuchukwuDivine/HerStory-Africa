## About This Opportunity

The program is a partnership between ICGEB, the Department of Science, Technology and Innovation, and the National Research Foundation (NRF) of South Africa. It offers a two-year postdoctoral position in a top-class international scientific environment at ICGEB Cape Town in specified macro areas of biotechnology.

## What You Get

- Two-year Postdoctoral Fellowship at ICGEB Cape Town
- Non-taxable stipend of ZAR 320,000 per annum and research costs of ZAR 50,000 per annum
- Additional mobility support to visit other ICGEB Components in Trieste, Italy and New Delhi, India plus mentoring and peer-to-peer learning

## Eligibility

- Female applicants only who are South African citizens or South African Permanent Residents with a valid South African ID number
- Hold a PhD degree in Biotechnology or a related discipline
- Preference given to Black and disabled applicants

## How to Apply

Contact the Principal Investigator or Group Leader at the chosen ICGEB Cape Town laboratory in advance to discuss the proposed research project and obtain a mandatory letter of endorsement. Apply via [NRF Connect](https://nrfconnect.nrf.ac.za) under NRF Postdoctoral Grants, indicating 'ICGEB' as the institution.
