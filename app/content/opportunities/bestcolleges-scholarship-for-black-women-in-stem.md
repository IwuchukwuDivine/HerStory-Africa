## About This Opportunity

This scholarship is for individuals who identify as a cisgender, transgender, or gender non-conforming Black woman, and who are pursuing a STEM-related field.

## What You Get

- $6,000 scholarship

## Eligibility

- Must be a legal resident of the 50 United States and the District of Columbia
- Must intend to enroll or be enrolled as an undergraduate or graduate student in an accredited college or university in the United States for the current academic year
- Must be at least 17 years of age
- Must identify as a cisgender, transgender, or gender non-conforming Black woman
- Must be pursuing a STEM-related field

## How to Apply

Visit the BestColleges website to learn more and apply.
