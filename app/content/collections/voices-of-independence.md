## About This Collection

Independence was never handed to Africa. It was demanded, fought for, and won — and women were at the centre of every liberation movement on the continent. These women organised protests, smuggled information, led spiritual resistance, and built the political infrastructure that made independence possible.

Many of their names were deliberately left out of official histories. This collection puts them back where they belong.
