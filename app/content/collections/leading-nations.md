## About This Collection

These women didn't just participate in politics — they led it. As heads of state, finance ministers, and directors of global institutions, they shaped policy that affected millions. Some inherited crises and stabilised nations. Others broke through ceilings that had never been cracked.

Their stories are proof that when women lead, communities and countries are transformed.
