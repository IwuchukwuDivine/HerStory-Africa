## About This Collection

These women proved that brilliance has no gender. In laboratories, classrooms, and hospitals across the continent, they produced groundbreaking research, trained the next generation of scientists, and used their expertise to protect public health. Some fought corruption in pharmaceutical regulation. Others stopped epidemics.

Their achievements are a blueprint for what becomes possible when women are given access to education and opportunity.
